Smart Travel Groups
Team Research + Design

170 respondents, 5 interviews, 8 platforms analysed. The hardest part of group travel is not finding a destination. It is committing to people. An 8-step confidence-building system built around that insight. This is a 7 member group project.

0-to-1 Build

Started with an AI prioritization hypothesis. Research killed it. Pivoted to behavioral re-entry support. Built a 4-state recovery model, chose recovery speed over completion rate as the north star.
